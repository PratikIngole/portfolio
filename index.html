About me

More than just code.

I'm Alex — a full-stack developer who believes software should feel inevitable. Not clever for its own sake. Not over-engineered. Just exactly right.

My journey started with a broken PHP forum theme and a 56k modem. Since then I've shipped production systems for fintech startups, e-commerce platforms handling millions in GMV, and a handful of side projects that taught me more than any job ever could.

Outside the terminal I shoot 35mm film, contribute to open-source, and argue (gently) that CSS is actually elegant when you understand it.
